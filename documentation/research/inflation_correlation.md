# Research Note: Inflation Correlation

## Event

Periods of rising CPI and their relationship to rates, equities, and commodities.

## Why This Matters

Inflation is central to the MacroLens thesis because it links multiple classes of economic indicators.

## Questions

- which datasets most consistently move with CPI changes
- what lag structure appears between oil, CPI, and policy rates
- where does the system confuse coincidence with real economic linkage

## Expected Signals

- lagged relationship between commodity pressure and CPI
- relationship between CPI changes and rate decisions
- weaker same-day alignment because macro effects often unfold slowly

## Value to the Project

This note helps test whether MacroLens can handle slower-moving macro relationships, not just sudden crashes.
